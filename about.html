College Tours,

Reimagined

With admissions to elite colleges becoming more and more competitive, it is crucial to have a personalized essay that demonstrates interest and fit. IvyScope helps you find the right colleges through immersive tours, so that you can craft a personal essay that will impress admissions officers.

Our Founders

Tian

Tian enjoys tutoring SAT/ACT and met many high school students who are eager to learn more about college life. Generic campus tours often fail to give high school applicants a sense of what it is like living on campus. We believe current undergrads have the potential to play host and introduce college life to high school students in a personalized and genuine way, without the sugarcoating.
